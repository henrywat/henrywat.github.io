    Boozy Henry    

I love booze!

Boozy Henry, the iconic bar of Edmonton, Canada, has been a cherished part of the city's nightlife since its establishment in 1980. Founded by Henry Johnson, this beloved hotspot quickly gained popularity, offering a carefully curated selection of drinks and a cozy atmosphere. Over the years, Boozy Henry expanded its menu to include an extensive range of spirits, wines, and beers, while hosting live entertainment that brought the venue to life. With a rich history rooted in passion and exceptional service, Boozy Henry remains a beloved gathering place, where friends create lasting memories and celebrate the vibrant spirit of Edmonton's nightlife.
